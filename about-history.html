History

Through outstanding research results, LIFAair has made significant contributions to Finnish indoor air quality standards. Established in 1988 in Helsinki, LIFAair specializes in indoor air purification technology and has spent more than 30 years in air pollution control, working with global government agencies, healthcare professionals and sporting organizations to improve the fundamental basic need for good indoor air quality.

 

Today, LIFAair uses advanced cleaning technology for consumer products to improve the overall wellness of families with a strong commitment to our ethos "Responsible for Every Breath".

 

In 2015, LIFAair entered into a joint venture with renowned manufacturer, Edifier Manufacturing Limited, expanding its global footprint into China while continuing to grow core markets in Europe, Asia Pacific and Oceania with the release of the company’s latest in smart home technologies, with a focus on home and personal needs.  

 

At IFA 2016, LIFAair brought its patented technology to the home environment with the launch of two new models LA352 and LA502 respectively, for home and personal use.

 

In 2017, LIFAair was awarded an iF Design award for the LA502 air purifier, firmly establishing the brand at the forefront of air purification systems.

Today, LIFAair manufactures the following products within the healthcare and wellness sector :

  • Air Purifiers
  • Air Cleaners as standalone units and/or integrated with HVAC-systems
  • Smart Controllers (Realtime AQI Readers)
  • Car Air Purifiers
  • Fresh Air Purifiers
  • Smart Air Purifying Ashtrays
  • Medical-grade Respirators
