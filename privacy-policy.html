Privacy Policy

LIFA AIR is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This Statement of Privacy applies to the LIFA AIR Web site and governs data collection and usage. By using the LIFA AIR website, you consent to the data practises described in this statement.

Collection of Personal Information
LIFA AIR collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. LIFA AIR also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favourites.

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by LIFA AIR. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by LIFA AIR for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of service and to provide general statistics regarding use of the LIFA AIR Web site.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or sensitive data through LIFA AIR public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: LIFA AIR does not read any of your private online communications.

LIFA AIR encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from LIFA AIR so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. LIFA AIR is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of AIRPULSE Web site.

Use of Personal Information
LIFA AIR collects and uses personal information to operate the LIFA AIR Web site and deliver services optimised for your best viewing experience. LIFA AIR also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from LIFA AIR and its affiliates. LIFA AIR may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

LIFA AIR does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. LIFA AIR may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to any third parties. In addition, LIFA AIR may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to AIRPULSE PRO, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

 

LIFA AIR does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

 

LIFA AIR keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within LIFA AIR, in order to determine what LIFA AIR services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customised content and advertising within LIFA AIR to customers whose behaviour indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

 

LIFA AIR Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on LIFA AIR or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of LIFA AIR; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of LIFA AIR, or the public.

Use of Cookies
The LIFA AIR Web site use "cookies" to help you personalise your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalise LIFA AIR pages, or register with LIFA AIR site or services, a cookie helps LIFA AIR to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same LIFA AIR Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the LIFA AIR features that you customised.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the LIFA AIR services or Web sites you visit.

Security of your Personal Information
LIFA AIR secures your personal information from unauthorised access, use or disclosure. LIFA AIR secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorised access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.

It explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, who we may disclose it to and your rights regarding personal data.
